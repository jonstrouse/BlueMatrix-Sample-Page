Established in 1999, the BlueMatrix ecosystem is the gold standard solution and the world's largest publishers of investment research. We connect the world's leading asset and wealth managers with over 1000 research contributors.

  • Revolutionary Authoring Platform: One stop shop. BlueMatrix's web editor creates PDFs, HTML5, Email and RIXML in one centralized authoring workflow.
  • A Full Suite of Complementary Module: Client Manager CRM to manage contact subscriptions to research, a device-responsive library, robust readership analytics platform are just a few of the applications that come with the package.
  • Compliance is a no brainer for us: BlueMatrix has powerful compliance tools that allow workflows to get extra scrutiny when content is subject to regulatory issues.
  • Support is our special sauce: Live phone support and personalized online ticket responses offer unprecedented service when things don't go as planned. Or if you just have a question. We're there along the way.
